About Me
I am Riya Sanket Kashive. I am an engineer by education, designer in practice and a maniacal polymath at heart, endlessly fascinated by others (and my own) idiosyncrasies. This makes Human Computer Interaction - particularly HAI - my happy place for deep dives.

As of writing, I am a final year student at IIT Kanpur, majoring in Civil Engineering and minoring in Computer Systems, Machine Learning, Linguistics and Cognitive Science. During my time at IITK, I have volunteered at Shiksha Sopan Aashram, and also run Outreach Cell and SIGCHI IITK. Last summer, I interned at Deutsche Bank. Had a blast there, and was lucky to receive a PPO.

I speak English, Hindi, a little Marathi and Duolingo-accented German. When I am not rushing to meet deadlines, I read, write, sing, doodle and play badminton :)
